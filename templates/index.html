Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hospital and services

General visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. ICU visiting is permitted twice daily for 15 minutes (11:00 AM and 6:00 PM). Special arrangements can be made for critical patients on request. We recommend checking with the nursing station for any department-specific guidelines.

SGS Hospital has tie-ups with most major insurance providers in India. We recommend verifying your specific plan coverage with our insurance desk before admission. For cashless hospitalization, please bring your insurance card and pre-authorization documents.

Medical records can be requested from our Medical Records Department either in person or through written request. You'll need to present valid ID proof and patient details. Standard processing time is 24-48 hours, and nominal administrative charges may apply.

We maintain stringent COVID-19 protocols including mandatory masks, temperature screening, and controlled visitor policies. All staff are fully vaccinated, and high-touch areas are sanitized regularly. Separate pathways and isolation facilities are available for suspected cases to ensure patient safety.
